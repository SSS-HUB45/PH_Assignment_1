Shelter for a family

Apart from those stranded in the remote villages, 6,000 people have taken shelter at 56 centres in Sylhet City. Cry for relief is getting louder in Sylhet and Sunamganj as at least 40 lakh people remain stranded due to flood in the two districts and over 3 lakh of them have taken refuge at different shelters.

Education for a child

The monsoon floods that ravaged the Sylhet region have turned the studies of over 455,000 students in 1522 institutions uncertain as they lost their book. Almost 100 educational institutions in the flood-hit Upazilas under the Sylhet division. The low-lying areas of the district have already been flooded, rendering nearly 0.1 million (one lakh) people marooned.




Our Agenda


We are trying our best to help the flood victims inshallah.

  • Floating medic boat providing healthcare and medication for stranded/marooned families and those stuck in shelters without access to medical care.
  • Identification of the most remote and vulnerable places where aid and other service are inadequate.
  • Utilizing the medic boat for emergency referrals such as mothers in labor including a hotline.
  • Supporting surveillance activities to identify potential disease outbreaks.
